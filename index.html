THE STAR WARS SURVEY

A survey conducted in 2014 using SurveyMonkey Audience was made on Star Wars movies and about 1188 people were surveyed.

FiveThirtyEight

Link to CSV Spreadsheet provided:

StarWars.csv

Downloading the CSV provided by FiveThirtyEight, and using LibraOffice and Google Sheets, I looked over the information provided from the survey to create my own charts based on different questions about the Star Wars movies.

ON TO THE QUESTIONS

1)How many people viewed Luke Skywalker favorably compared to unfavorably and unfamiliar?

Out of 831 respondents, 66.4% viewed Luke Skywalker favorably while 0.4% viewed him as very unfavorably and only 0.7% were unfamiliar with him.

2)The same question could be asked for Jar Jar Binks. 821 respondentss were surveyed and 25.8% unfavored him very much compared to only 13.6% who favored him

3)What were the percentages of the genders of those surveyed and their ages?

For how many femlaes compared to males that were surveyed, 549 females came in at 54.4% while 497 males came in at 47.4%

4)As for the age ranges, for 1046 respondents, 20.8% were in the 18-29 age range, 25.6% were in the 30-44 age group, 27.8% were in the 45-60 group and those over 60 years old made up with 25.7%

As for the final question, how many of those surveyed have seen any of the six movies. 1186 people responded and it was 78.9% of those who yes have seen any of the six films while 21.1% have not.
