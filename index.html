THE STAR WARS SURVEY

A survey conducted in 2014 using SurveyMonkey Audience was made on Star Wars movies and about 1188 people were surveyed.

FiveThirtyEight

Using LibraOffice and Google Sheets, I looked over the information provided from the survey to create my own charts based on different questions about the Star Wars movies.

ON TO THE QUESTIONS

1)How many people viewed Luke Skywalker favorably compared to unfavorably and unfamiliar?

Out of 831 respondents, 66.4% viewed Luke Skywalker favorably while 0.4% viewed him as very unfavorably and only 0.7% were unfamiliar with him.

2)The same question could be asked for Jar Jar Binks. 821 respondentss were surveyed and 25.8% unfavored him very much compared to only 13.6% who favored him
